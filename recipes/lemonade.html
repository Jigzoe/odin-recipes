Lemonade

Recipe for a simple lemonade

Lemonade is a great refreshing drink for hot summers

Ingredients

  • Lemon - 4 to 5
  • Ice cubes - 1/2 cup
  • Sugar - 4 tbsp
  • Chilled Water - 1/2 cup
  • Vodka - 1 shot

Steps

  1. Cut and squeeze the lemons in to a cup
  2. Combine all of the ingredients
  3. Stir and serve
